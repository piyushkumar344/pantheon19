  • Instructions

    • Update: Team size 5 is allowed.
    • Note: Team once created, will not be modified.


    • Each of the team members must have completed their registration before registering for any event. Once you complete your registration, you will receive a Pantheon ID which you need to enter here for registering a team.
    • Every user can be a part of only one team.
    • A user can either Create/Join a team.
    • To create a team, head over to "Team" tab. If you create the team, you will be the team leader and will be able to register in events on behalf of the team. A team can only consist of 5-8 members. Choose a funky but unique team name. Otherwise, ask your friend to add you to their team..
    • After registering your team, the Events tab will be unlocked and you will be provided with a unique Team ID.


    • There is onsite registration for participation in all Formal and Informal Events.
    • The registration for each flagship event is separate. The links for the same can be found on the "Events" tab.
    • The team members may belong to the same or different colleges. Upon winning an event, the team will be awarded some points based on the event.
    • Points are awarded to the top 3 Teams for all Formal, Informal and Flagship events. If the team members participate and win, the points will be added to their overall team score.
    • The overall winner will be the team scoring maximum points.


    • The Last Date for registering a Team is 26th September. ( Dates are tentative )
    • If you have any query or run into some error, Mail us at - pantheonbitfest@gmail.com
