CUBE-DE-CEMENTO


Event Description

If you get attracted towards composed materials especially cement and concrete, Cube-de-Cemento will definitely grab your attention. You will get a chance to showcase your concrete skills and probably make your first cube made of cement! You would surely not want to miss this opportunity.

Day:   1

Time:   11:00 AM

Venue:  LH1

Points:  250-200-150

For queries contact:- 




Rules and Regulations

  • The first one will be a time-bound preliminary elimination round where the contestants have to crack aptitude problems.
  • Based on the performance of participants in the aptitude round, a total of 12 teams will be selected for the second round.Â 
  • In the second round, each team will be given a few kgs of cement along with some chips and sand to prepare a concrete cube. The given substances will be in the ratio of 1:2:3.
  • The quality of the cubes like the tensile strength and concreteness will decide the winning team. The team which ends up with a cube having correct proportion of cement, chips and sand will be adjudged as winners.
