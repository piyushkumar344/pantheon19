DROID TROOPER


Event Description

The teams have to build a manual robot (wired or wireless) which has to pick-up cubes from their respective location points and place them on a rotating disc. During the process it should cross through rough terrains which is divided into multiple zones. Different zones contain different obstacles. Teams need to design their robot in such a way that it can place the cubes correctly at the target locations and cross all the obstacles to successfully complete the race. The teams will be awarded points for the above task and also for crossing the zones in the stipulated time limit.

Day:   1

Time:   11:00 AM
