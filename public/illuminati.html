ILLUMINATI


Event Description

Illuminati, the mock-paper presentation event of Pantheon’19 brings to you a once-in-a-while opportunity to unleash your creative side. If you have the mettle to prove your technical prowess, Illuminati provides you with the perfect platform to present your research paper on a scientific theme. All you have to do prior to the presentation is submit your abstract and relevant information set. Are you ready to enlighten and establish a new world order?

Theme

"In the census 2011, a person aged seven and above, who can both read and write with understanding in any language is treated as literate. A person, who can only read but can’t write, is not literate. India's literacy rate is at 74.04%. Kerala is the most literate state in India, with 93.91% literacy. Bihar is the least literate state in India, with a literacy of 63.82%."

What according to you are the reasons for such a low literacy rate? Why is there such a stark difference between the literacy rates among the states, even after 'Education for All' being a fundamental human right.

Rules and Regulations

  • Participating teams will have to prepare a report of their Presentation with required graphic details, in less than 1000 words, and mail it to the address of eventsteam.pantheon@gmail.com
  • The selected teams will be sent mails regarding their selection and will be informed about the further rounds.
  • The teams will be given 15 minutes to present their research paper with a supplementary PowerPoint presentation.
  • The selected teams have to submit at least five hard copies of their research to the esteemed panel who will be scrutinizing their research.
  • There will be an additional viva session of every team at the end of their presentations.
  • The teams will be judged on their content, level of research, presentation skills and viva performances.
  • The dress code will be strictly formal wears.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the Judging Panel will be final and binding.
