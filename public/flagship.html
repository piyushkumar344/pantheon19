Event Description

Paper Presentation, in its most unadulterated and genuine form comes to Pantheon in the form of Illuminati. To try and test your technical prowess, and provide you with a judgement of your presentation skills, Illuminati offers you the opportunity to present a research paper on a scientific theme. All you have to do prior to the presentation is submit your abstract and relevant information set. Illuminate and thou shalt pass.

Day:   1

Time:   11:00 AM

Venue:  LH1

Points:  250-200-150

For queries contact:- 




Rules and Regulations

  • Participating teams will have to prepare a report of their Presentation with required graphic details, in less than 1000 words, and mail it to the address of events@pantheon19.in before the 10th of October.
  • The selected teams will be sent mails regarding their selection and will be informed about the further rounds.
  • The teams will be given 15 minutes to present their research paper with a supplementary PowerPoint presentation.
  • The selected teams have to submit at least five hard copies of their research to the esteemed panel who will be scrutinizing their research.
  • There will be an additional viva session of every team at the end of their presentations.
  • The teams will be judged on their content, level of research, presentation skills and viva performances.
  • The dress code will be strictly formal wears.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the Judging Panel will be final and binding.
