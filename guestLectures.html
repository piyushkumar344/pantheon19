Lecture 2

Name:   Dr. Anandmayee Tej, IIST Kerala


Topic:   A Journey to the stars: Unraveling the mysteries


Venue:   SH-2


Day:   1


Time:   11.30 AM-12.30 PM



Event Description

"Though my soul may set in darkness, it will rise in perfect light; I have loved the stars too fondly to be fearful of the night." As the allure of the vast space is sending scintillating chills down your spine, Pantheon is all set to intensify this magnificent quest of intrigue and mystique. With this very thought in mind, Pantheon’19 enthusiastically announces its 2nd guest lecturer, Dr. Anandmayee Tej. An esteemed Professor at IIST, Kerala, Dr. Tej is currently working to expand the horizons of High Angular Resolution Astronomy. With specialisation in Particle Physics, he has an exceptional experience of being a visiting scientist at Space Telescope Science Institute, Baltimore, USA. From stellar population studies to AGB stars, he is looking forward to a major breakthrough in the field of astronomy any time soon now. Make sure to catch him live as he mesmerizes you all on 'A journey to the stars: Unravelling Mysteries' and elucidates us with his take on the breathtaking uncharted realms of 'Stellar Systems'.

Lecture 1

Name:   Dr. Soumen Mondal, Satyendra Nath Bose National Center for Basic Sciences


Topic:   Galactic Star Forming


Venue:   SH-2


Day:   2


Time:   10.30 AM-11.30 AM



Event Description

Pantheon’19 is ecstatic to announce that Dr. Soumen Mondal, Associate Professor and the Head of the Department in SNBNCBS, is going to be our 3rd guest lecturer. An alumnus of Jadavpur University, he completed his Ph.D. in Physics from Physical Research Laboratory, Gujarat University. Dr. Mondal has also served as a scientist in ARIES, Nainital for four years. A prolific publisher of a number of research papers, his areas of expertise revolve around but are not confined to extra-solar planets, small solar system objects, brown dwarfs, high angular astronomy, and astronomical instrumentation. Be there to witness this erudite personality not only imparting his wisdom on 'Galactic star formation' and 'Pre-main Sequence' but also taking you on a cosmic expedition to the stars and beyond.
